Chelsea

Hello, I'm Chelsea. I grew up in the mountains of eastern Washington, near the Canadian border. My parents moved to eastern Oregon just a week before I left on my mission to the Philippines. I came home to a lot of changes. There, I met my husband of 17 years. We have 4 wild kids and now live in Idaho.

Five years ago my husband had some health concerns which made to me realize if something were to happen to him, I have no way to support myself or my children so I decided as soon as my youngest started school, I would get the degree I hadn't finish before getting married. So, here I am!

I am nervous for this semester because, while I did well on my class last semester, at the 5th and 6th week mark of the block class, I was putting in 40+ hours a week for the one class. It was super stressful on me and my family. However, I've learned a lot and am excited to continue my education in Computer and Web Design.
