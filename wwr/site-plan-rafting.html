Overview

Purpose

Here at the Dry Oar we want to make our business available to those in all walks of life who wish to go rafting. This site allows you to see your options and get a feel for what type of rafting and what equiptment you'd like to try before purchasing your own.

Audience

This site is geared towards the "outdoorsy person". Such person will want it to feel like it's outdoorsy but possibly including water and trees/greenery colors as that is usually the theme of someone rafting. Simple but catchy fonts and more nuteral colors that are calm and inviting and this should be made available for all devices.
