Content

Home page

Here at NaiNai Leather & Creations, we offer unprecedented and hand-crafted leatherwork and custom graphic designs. Have you ever gone to a craft show and been drawn into the beautiful leather, hand-made items? They all claimed to be hand crafted and unique. But have you ever picked up a piece and look it over and thought, "No doubt it was beautiful but it was obvious to even an untrained eye that this is most definitely NOT hand crafted"? Well, perhaps the staining or painting was authentic but the actual leatherwork wasn't. Then, you flipped the tag over and had to pick your jaw up off the floor, seeing the astronomical prices? Yes, the piece was real leather but the bragged about handtooling wasn't and they were pricing it as if it were. Knowing what life’s like on a farm, and understanding the reality of having to scrimp for good quality stuff but also knowing how important it is to have good, long-lasting quality, and having been raised around leather work but never had time to get into it seriously, got me thinking, I wonder if I could make high-quality, hand-crafted items for a reasonable price. After a few years of practicing, people started noticing the quality and uniqueness and wanted me to make stuff for them and NaiNai Leather & Creations was born. The love making a genuine product that are strikingly beautiful, with unmatched designs all while keeping it at a price that the honest hard-working American can afford.

Graphic Prints

When ask what other products people would like to see available, there were many remarks on wanting clothing items that depict the real western lifestyles of the customer without requiring them to change or settle for cookie-cutter, factory produced products available in bulk. From this, NaiNai Leather & Creations started a graphics line. Unlike many graphic companies who simply copy and paste an image off the internet, NaiNai Leather & Creations sees an image or takes a picture of a scene then, goes home and creates the image from scratch and ends up with digital creations that are truly one of a kind. These graphics are then printed onto any number of materials, giving not only unique styles of clothing but also unprecedented images on the clothing. If you are looking for something that is one of a kind, hand crafted from scratch, and that will be the talk of…well, wherever you’ll be, and that won’t leave a hole in your wallet, this is where you need to shop. NaiNai’s takes custom orders on just about anything and gives you your money’s worth and then some.
